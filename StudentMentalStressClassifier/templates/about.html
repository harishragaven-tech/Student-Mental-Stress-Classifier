About MindCheck

MindCheck is an AI-powered tool built to help students understand their mental stress levels using a Support Vector Machine (SVM) classifier trained on real student survey data.

Data-Driven

Trained on a dataset of student lifestyle, sleep, anxiety, and academic factors.

SVM Classifier

Uses a Support Vector Machine to classify stress into Low, Moderate, or High.

Personalized Tips

Provides coping suggestions tailored to your predicted stress level.

Private & Local

Your responses are stored locally in SQLite for analysis, not shared externally.

Built for Students

Designed as an academic project to raise awareness about student mental health.
